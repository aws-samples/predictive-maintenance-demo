# Specs for building

## Materials

1. Laser cut box
2. 3D printed motor holder
3. Foam
4. 12V DC Motor
5. Motor speed controller
6. 6V DC Vibration Motor
7. Motor driver

9. RS485 Hat
10. Vibration Sensor
11. Mounting for vibration sensor
12. Power supply, 24 V
13. Voltage converter 24V1.5V
14. SD Card
15. On/off switch
16. USB cable
17. Power Cable
18. Power Connector
19. Alarm light
20. Arduino
21. Button from Arduino Started kit
